About GNDEC

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), Ludhiana, was established in 1956 by the Nankana Sahib Education Trust. It is one of the oldest and most respected engineering colleges in North India. The college was founded to promote technical education for students from rural and weaker sections.

The college is affiliated to I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and is an autonomous institution approved by AICTE and UGC.

Departments

GNDEC has several engineering and technical departments offering undergraduate and postgraduate degrees:

  • Computer Science Engneering(CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical Engineering (EE)

Academic Programs

GNDEC offers a wide variety of programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels:

  • B.Tech: 4-year programs in various streams
  • M.Tech: Advanced specialization in engineering fields
  • Other: MBA, MCA, B.Arch, B.Voc, BBA, BCA
  • Ph.D.: Available through QIP scheme
